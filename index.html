Hi, I'm Yutong Yuan, a graduate student studying data journalism at University of Missouri. I love finding stories in data and telling them through graphics. I'm a true believer of the magic of data viz and a constant learner of digital tools.

A journaism major at college, I explored radio broadcasting, video shooting, and reporting through courses, school newspaper and radio station. My first internship as a business reporter after collge led me to the world of data. It also inspired me to study at Mizzou and to pursue my career as a data journalist.

At Mizzou, I'm learning by doing. The experience of working with the Missourian, a local newspaper with a history of 110 years, as a reporter and a graphic designer let me understand how to tell stories and how to work under the pressure of deadlines. At the same time I get the chance to hone my the skills of data-cleaning, data-analysis and data visualization in a real setting.
