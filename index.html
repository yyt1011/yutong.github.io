Hi, I'm Yutong Yuan, a graduate student studying data journalism at University of Missouri.

I love finding stories in data and telling them through graphics. I'm a true believer of the magic of data viz and a constant learner of digital tools.

At Mizzou, I'm learning by doing. The experience of working with the Missourian, a local newspaper, as a reporter and a graphic designer let me understand how to tell stories, how to work under the pressure of deadlines. and it also allow me to hone my the skills of data-cleaning, data-analysis and data visualization in a real setting.
